About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, where I specialize in medical applications of AI and machine learning. My research includes secured remote ECG monitoring and accelerated CEST MRI acquisition in collaboration with cardiologists and radiologists.

Previously, I received the Best Research Award at the IEEE CCWC 2024 and the Best Presenter Award at the University of Nebraska Omaha 2024 Research and Creative Activity Fair. Before starting my Ph.D., I completed my B.S. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering with honors at Istanbul University.
